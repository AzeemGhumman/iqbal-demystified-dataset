*
از  ن غم ہا دل ما دردمند است
کہ اصل او ازین خاک نژند است
من و تو زان غم شیرین ندانیم
کہ اصل او ز افکار بلند است 
*
VI-VI
*


*
Such griefs this heart now likes to take,
Like dust of low things of humble make.
To those sweet griefs alas! we know not,
Which make a man great with higher ‘thought. $1

*
1.         Khak-i-Nazand (Per) Dust which is low, downcast, sorrowful, blind, (as such this world). (See text). Sweet griefs, of God and country's love, etc.
