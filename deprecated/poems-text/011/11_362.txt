کہ دلھا زندہ گردد از دم مرد
نگہدارد ز  ہ و نالہ خود را
کہ خود دار است چون مردان ، غم مرد
*
I-XVII
*


*
You cant learn aught ‘sans’ $1 a conscious soul,
The hearts get a life from breath of man’s dole $2 .
Who guards his ego by wails and sighs,
And keeps self’s honour by ruthful eyes.

*
1.         Sans, (Sanz) Fr without. a conscious soul; viz, a guide.

2.         Dole, n., pain, grief. (arch and in poetry), heaviness of heart. Hence doleful., adj.
