کبوتر بچہ خود را چہ خوش گفت
*
کبوتر بچہ خود را چہ خوش گفت
کہ نتوان زیست با خوی حریری
اگر ’’یاہو‘‘ زنی از مستی شوق
کلہ را از سر شاہین بگیری
*
III-IV
*


*
A gull said to shaver, nice witty thing,
You cannot exist with the silken wing,
With a fondness great if you call “Ya Hoo” $1
You can catch hawk’s head with a loving coo.

*
1.         'Ya Hoo' (Hu); O GOd (O! who is God). Some people say that a Pigeon says 'Ya Hoo' in every coo. Hence Iqbal is speaking in passages. His pigeon is a virtuous man. In America a pigeon is also called a gull (uor in Britain) kullah; (Per) The skull head, the pate.
