کہ گفت او را کہ آید بوی یاری؟
*
کہ گفت او را کہ آید بوی یاری؟
کہ داد او را امید نوبہاری؟
چون آن سوز کہن رفت از دم او
کہ زد بر نیستان او شراری؟
*
XI-LXVI
*


*
Who told him I smell thee ‘under the rose’,
To give him good news of a spring tide close.
When I saw not in him thy old flame’s blaze,
With a new spark I set his caneswood ablaze. $1

*
Here Iqbal is addressing the Holy Prophet, "I have given them (my nation) a new hope to receive the Prophet's flame of love inspite of the fact that She is passing though a process of decline, I have told them not to be disappointed."
