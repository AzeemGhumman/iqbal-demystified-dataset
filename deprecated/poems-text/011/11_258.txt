نگہ دارد برہمن کار خود را
*
نگہ دارد برہمن کار خود را
نمی گوید بہ کس اسرار خود را
بمن گوید کہ از تسبیح بگذر
بدوش خود برد زنار خود را
*
XIX-III
*


*
A pundit keeps eyes on his own task,
He wont let to know his secret mask.
To me he says Give up counting beads,
For his own neck feels holy thread’s need. $1

*
1.         Counting beads, (tasbeeh) viz. the rosary notes. Brahmen's sacred thread; a rosary too, is called zunnar in Persian and janaou in Hindi or Sanskrit. The Muslim keeps a rosary of 100 or 101 beads for counting purpose whereas the Christian keeps a rosary of 165 beads to keep a count.
