دل صاحبدلان او برد یا من؟
*
دل صاحبدلان او برد یا من؟
پیام شوق او آورد یا من ؟
من و ملا ز کیش دین دو تیریم 
بفرما بر ہدف او خورد یا من؟
*
XI-XLI
*


*
The heart of lucent hearts he took or I? $1
Thy message of love did he brought or I?
Two shafts of Deen’s quiver are ‘mullah’ and I,
Who took the right aim: did ‘mullah’ or I.

*
1.         The underlying thought is given below in a verse: [Two shafts of envy are Mullah and I, Who took the right step did Mullah or I?] Here shaft means arrow or spear. (in figurative sense) as shafts of envy (or ridicule), means expressions of envy.
