مسلمان را ہمین عرفان و ادراک
*
مسلمان را ہمین عرفان و ادراک
کہ در خود فاش بیندر مزلولاک
خدا اندر قیاس مانگنجد
شناس  نرا کہ گوید ما عرفناک
*
IV-V
*


*
For Muslim ‘this is the gnosis and ken,
In self he sees vivid Laulak’s $1 ken when.
We cannot hold God as in our thought,
Be conscious of whom who said, ‘you knew not’. $2

*
1.         Laulaka; Lamma Khuliqat-il-Aflak (A Hadith) If you had not been the whole skies would have not been.

2.         Ma'araf na ka (haqqa marifatika), We admit O God that we did not fulfill the debt and duty of Thy gnosis (acquaintance). M'arifat, knowledge gained through experience, enough for recognition, but not as deep as certitude.
