چو موج از بحر خود بالیدہ ام من
*
چو موج از بحر خود بالیدہ ام من
بخود مثل گہر پیچیدہ ام من
از آن نمرود با من سر گران است
بہ تعمیر حرم کوشیدہ ام من
*
I-II
*


*
My self’s own sea gave a rise to me,
It sharpen’d my wits like pearls in sea.
On me that ‘Nimrod’ is boiling with rage.
Fm trying to build up ‘Harem’s’ image. $1

*
1.         Bakhud paecheedgan; According to a contemporary scholar it means to fortify and cultivate one's own self; strong thing of one's own ego. I also understand its meanings are self seeing (though literal meanings are self-writhing's Self coiling viz, the philosophers and other thinkers. Nimrods viz, the Nimrods and Pharoahs (Feros) of this age, the enemies of faith. Baleedan, to grow, to rise.
