طلسم علم حاضر را شکستم
*
طلسم علم حاضر را شکستم
ربودم دانہ و دامش گسستم
خدا داند کہ مانند براہیم
بہ نار او چہ بے پروا نشستم
*
XI-L
*


*
I broke the magic of the modern age,
I pounced on the grains and broke the trap cage.
The God knows that in the Abram’s wake,
How fearless I sat in that fire’s stake. $1

*
1.         Abraham or Abram, dim; Abe; (for lbrahim). Stake, a post to which a person was tied, in olden days before being burned to death as a punishment for heresy.
