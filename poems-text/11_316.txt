جہان مھر و مہ زناری اوست
*
جہان مھر و مہ زناری اوست
گشاد ھر گرہ از زاری اوست
پیامی دہ ز من ہندوستان را
غلام ،  زاد از بیداری اوست
*
VIII-VI
*


*
The world of Sun and Moon, slave of his thread,
Each tie will be ope from tears lie can shed.
To ‘Hind’ please convey a message of me,
When a slave is wide awake he is free. $1

*
1.         Wide awake, adj (fig:) fully alert, vigilant. Zunnar is a thread (with hundred of ties) worn by Hindu Brahmins or Pundits as a religious custom, (worn around neck).
