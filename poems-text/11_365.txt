قماش و نقرہ و لعل و گہر چیست؟
*
قماش و نقرہ و لعل و گہر چیست؟
غلام خوشگل و زرین کمر چیست؟
چو یزدان از دو گیتی بی نیازند
دگر سرمایۂ اہل ہنر چیست؟
*
I-XX
*


*
What are the clothes, $1 gold $2 jewels $3 and gems? $4
Rows of slaves handsome, and gold waist femme?
Like God they ‘are free’ $5 from both worlds needs,
Is this not wealth which a skill’d man $6 heeds.

*
1.         Qumash, the clothing.

2.         Nuqra, Silver (I used gold for poetic need).

3.         La'l; a ruby, red jewel

4.         Guhr; gems.

5.         Are free; Sans souci, unconcerned.

6.         Here skilled man means a godly man, virtuous.
