خیالش با مہ و انجم نشیند
*
خیالش با مہ و انجم نشیند
نگاہش  ن سوی پروین ببیند
دل بیتاب خود را پیش او نہ
دم او رعشہ از سیماب چیند
*
V-VII
*


*
His thought thus flies with stars and moon rays,
His eye thus views beyond milky ways.
Lay thy restive heart at his fluid tune,
From his quick silver get a quick calm soon. $1

*
1.         When you shake the thermometer the mercury settles down. It was difficult to translate the last line of this twin couplet.
