بگو از من نواخوان عرب را
*
بگو از من نواخوان عرب را
بہای کم نھادم لعل لب را
از  ن نوری کہ از قر ن گرفتم
سحر کردم صد و سی سالہ شب را
*
TO THE ARAB POET
CHAPTER 7
*


*
To Arab $1 poets sweet on my part say,
I shun to versify on red lips gay.
From a beam I had of Holy Book’s light,
After hundred years nights I see a dawn bright

*
1.         Iqbal is advising Arab (pront as Arb also) poets to give uP love poetry (ode) of beautiful women as he was seeing a change to good after one hundred thirty years continuous decline of Arh world.
