چو رومی در حرم دادم اذان من
*
چو رومی در حرم دادم اذان من
ازو آموختم اسرار جان من
بہ دور فتنۂ عصر کہن ، او
بہ دور فتنۂ عصر روان من
*
XI-LXIII
*


*
Like Rumi I raised His call in Harem whole,
From whom I learnt secret knowledge of soul.
He passed through the crises of his time,
I am going too through crisis and crime. $1

*
1.         The simple and literal translation of line 3 and 4 is given below: [An old time of crisis he had seen, Through this age of crisis I have been]
