تو میگوئی کہ دل از خاک و خون است
*
تو میگوئی کہ دل از خاک و خون است
گرفتار طلسم کاف و نون است
دل ما گرچہ اندر سینۂ ماست
ولیکن از جھان ما برون است
*
VIII-V
*


*
You say the heart is the Khak and Khoon $1 ,
A work of magic of Kaf and Noon. $2
My heart is though in the bosom of mine,
It is yet beyond the pale of world thine.

*
1.         Khak and Khoon; mud (dust) and blood.

2.         K and N or Kaf and Nun, are two Arabic alphabets. From K and N, God said 'Kun' = be, become and the whole cosmos came into being. God says 'be' (Kun) and it 'becomes' automatically a reality.
