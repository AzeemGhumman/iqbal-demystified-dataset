یکی از حجرۂ خلوت برون  ی
*
یکی از حجرۂ خلوت برون  ی
بباد صبحگاہی سینہ بگشای
خروش این مقام رنگ و بو را
بقدر نالۂ مرغی بیفزای
*
I-II
*


*
Leave thy solitude cell for a while please,
Ope thy bosom thus from the morning breeze.
For a tempo to world’s hustle and thrill,
Raise thy tune and tone to song bird’s trill. $1

*
1.         1. Hustle, n., quick and energetic activity (compare with). Bustle. n., excited activity. (used in sing only). Trill. n. and v.t. and vi.; sing a (musical note) with a trill; trill. n, a shaking voice.
