بروے من در دل باز کردند
*
بروے من در دل باز کردند
ز خاک من جھانی ساز کردند
ز فیض او گرفتم اعتباری
کہ با من ماہ و انجم ساز کردند
*
V-VI
*


*
To me his heart’s door was always ope,
From my dust he caused a world’s new hope.
From his grace I got a grace and trust.
For me he tamed the Moon and Stars first. $1

*
1.         Moon and stars here it means the angels of fate (who are the proctors of fate as well) or those who are living heavens became my supporters.
