اگر این  ب و جاہی از فرنگ است
*
اگر این  ب و جاہی از فرنگ است
جبین خود منہ جز بر در او
سرین را ھم بچوبش دہ کہ  خر
حقی دارد بہ خر پالان گر او
*
III-VII
*


*
If this pomp and show the Anglian $1 boon,
Bow not thy forehead save at his shoon. $2
Get kicks of his stick at thy ass $3 too,
A right on the ass $4 of saddlers’ $5 pooh


*
1.         Anglians, adj of Anglia (England) of the angles, their culture, dialect etc.

2.         Shoon (archaic or dial.) p1: of shoe. Pooh (poo) (slang) an exclamation of disdain, hate or impatience.

3.         Ass (slang); the buttocks; a vulgar term.

4.         Here 'ass' means donkey.

5.         Saddlers=here it means pack saddlers.
