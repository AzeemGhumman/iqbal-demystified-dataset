بدہ او را جوان پاکبازی
*
بدہ او را جوان پاکبازی
سرورش از شراب خانہ سازی
قوی بازوی او مانند حیدر 
دل او از دو گیتی بی نیازی
*
XI-LIV
*


*
Bid him O lord! a holy man’s lead,
Who is proud of his ‘home made wine’s need. $1
Like Hyder, the Lion whose arms are strong,
For both world’s wealth he would never long.

*
1          Home made wine In Iqbal's own terminology this term applies to Islamic traditions, culture and civilization.
