گہی شعر عراقی را بخوانم
*
گہی شعر عراقی را بخوانم
گہی جامی زند آتش بجانم
ندانم گرچہ آہنگ عرب را
شریک نغمہ ہای ساربانم
*
VII-I
*


*
A page of Iraqi $1 sometimes I turn,
From Jami’s $2 fire so often I burn.
I know not though the Arabs’ tune,
I share with joy the teamaster’s $2 tune.

*
1.         Imam Fakhr-ud-Din known as Iraqi (of Iraq), a mystic poet.

2.         Jami Maulana Abdul Rehman Of Kharjrohmin, a town in Jam province of Persia, born in Sha'ban 817 H, died in 808 H in Muharram, wrote 54-books on various topics, some were text books in scholastic school Teamster; a driver of camels.
