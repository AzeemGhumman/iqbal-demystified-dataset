بہشتی بھر پاکان حرم ہست
*
بہشتی بھر پاکان حرم ہست
بہشتی بہر ارباب ہمم ہست
بگو ہندی مسلمان را کہ خوش باش
بہشتی فی سبیل اللہ ہم ہست
*
V-III
*


*
A paradise lies for the pious alone,
A paradise waits for the warriors $1 lone.
Tell the Hindi $2 Muslims to sing in glee,
There lies for them a paradise free.

*
1.         Warriors; (Liter and Rhet:) Soldier, a fighter. Otherwise use soldier or fighter in preference to Warriors. Arbab-I-Humam; men of courage, viz Warriors. Here Iqbal has made three categories of paradise in a sarcastic manner.

2.         Viz. Indians. This is a Sarcastic address to Indian Muslims.
