غریبم در میان محفل خویش
*
غریبم در میان محفل خویش
تو خود گو با کہ گویم مشکل خویش
از آن ترسم کہ پنہانم شود فاش
غم خود را نگویم با دل خویش
*
XI-XLII
*


*
An alien I am within my own race,
To whom I should take the ‘Issues’ I face.
Those hidden pricks I fear to disclose,
I tell not my griefs to heart, though close. $1

*
1.         At last Iqbal feels that none is the friend of a man in this world save 'Allah' alone. Iqbal explains this theme in another couplet. [To know this seeker everyone failed, To whom what he said, from where be hailed?]
