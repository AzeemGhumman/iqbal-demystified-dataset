قلندر جرہ باز  سمانہا
*
قلندر جرہ باز  سمانہا
بہ بال او سبک گردد گرانہا
فضای نیلگون نخچیر کاہش
نمی گردد بہ گرد  شیانہا
*
TO FRIENDS OF COMMON CREED (SAME PATH)
*


*
The Qalandar $1 is a bold hawk of sky,
A heavy weight to him so light like fly.
To him his hunting ground the whole blue space,
On dust he makes never his aery’s base. $2

*
1.         Iqbal's own sense of ghayur means jealous for honour, no one word in English carries this sense. Its synonym is 'a man of parts' (p1:) abilities, modest, noble, bashful plus chaste conveys the sense of 'ghayur'. Is not suitable in new satan's chart.
2.         Aery (eerie) the nest or brood of an eagle.
