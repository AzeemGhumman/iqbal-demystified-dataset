یکی بنگرد فرنگی کج کلاہان
*
یکی بنگرد فرنگی کج کلاہان
تو گوئی آفتابانند و ماہان
جوان سادہ من گرم خون است
نگہ دارش ازین کافر نگاہان
*
XII-II
*


*
Look to these saucy anglican maids,
As if Suns and moons have come for raids.
My simple young nation keeps a blood warm,
Beware! the heathen eye’s sensual storm $1


*
1.         The above quatrain (twin couplet) is addressed to his son Javed, when he was a few English girls in almost nude dress. Kaj Kullah also means beloved, a king, a title of Persian Kings prior to Muslim era.
