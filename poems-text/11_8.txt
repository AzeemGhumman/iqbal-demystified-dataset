بخود پیچیدگان در دل اسیرند
*
بخود پیچیدگان در دل اسیرند
ھمہ دردند و درمان ناپذیرند
سجود از ما چہ میخواہی کہ شاہان
خراجی از دہ ویران نگیرند
*
II-III
*


*
The self writhing $1 hearts are captives of lures,
All writhing in pains and wriggling for cures.
Thou seekest my kowtows but see that kings,
Are prone not to tax the desolated wings. $2

*
1.         Self writhing-for want of contentment.

2.         Desolated wings barren lands. His heart, as much his forehead are barren, as the light of God is not filled there in.
