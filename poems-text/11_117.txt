دل ملا گرفتار غمی نیست
*
دل ملا گرفتار غمی نیست
نگاہی ہست ، در چشمش نمی نیست
از آن بگریختم از مکتب او
کہ در ریگ حجازش زمزمی نیست
*
XI-XXXIX
*


* 
The ‘mullah’ never knows the pangs of grief,
No tears ever flow in his eyes and belief, $1
From his School of quacks I took to my heels,
The sounds of desert chants $2 his dust can’t feel.

*
1.         Line 2 (Literal); His eyes thus lack a moist of the grief.

2.         Zamzam Chant, here it means the sound of a chants of the caravans going to Holy land.
