مرا این سوز از فیض دم تست
*
مرا این سوز از فیض دم تست
بہ تاکم موج می از زمزم تست
خجل ملک جم از درویشی من
کہ دل در سینۂ من محرم تست
*
XI-LVII
*


*
To me this burning a boon of thy glow,
Thy font’s wine is waving in my vine so.
In shame the Join’s realm to my content bows,
With thee as my heart had tete-a-tete close. $1

*
1.         The underlying thought Iqbal says in a verse in Bal-e-Jibreel: [The Jum feels small at my content's fame, As my heart is beating in thy love's flame. To Dara and Eck that dervesh is great, In want who can keep a lion's pluck and trait. ]
