بیا از من بگیر  ن دیر سالہ
*
بیا از من بگیر  ن دیر سالہ
کہ بخشد روح با خاک پیالہ
اگر  بش دہی از شیشۂ من
قد  دم بروید شاخ لالہ
*
I-V
*


*
Come $1 and take from $2 me that old wine’s bowl.$3
Which gives the dust of bowl $4 quite a new soul,
If you water this poppy from my can,
A branch $5 will grow to the size of a man.



*
1.         Come=to join his path.

2.         Take from me learn.

3.         Old wine's bowl, viz, the lover's way.

4.         Dust of bowl; a hint or allusion to the lover's heart.

5.         A branch; or poppy's branch = the whole nation, the Ummah, or one man.
