*
بہ بند صوفی و ملا اسیری
حیات از حکمت قر ن نگیری 
بہ  یاتش ترا کاری جز این نیست
کہ از ’’یسن‘‘ او  سان بمیری
*
IV-III
*


*
To Mullah and Sufi thou art a slave,
From insight of Quran no life you crave.
You need verses only at time of grief,
That ‘Yasin’ would give death paugs a relief. $1

*
1.         The above mentioned twin couplet of Iqbal reminds another verse about the Mullah. He is bet in himself, how can he lead.
