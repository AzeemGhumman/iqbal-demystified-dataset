سکندر با خضر خوش نکتہ ئی گفت
*
سکندر با خضر خوش نکتہ ئی گفت
شریک سوز و ساز بحر و بر شو
تو این جنگ از کنار عرصہ بینی
بمیر اندر نبرد و زندہ تر شو
*
(33)
*

*
Sikandar $1 said to Khizar $2 aptly: "Dive
Into the stormy sea of life and strive
Against the waves. Why watch them from the shore?
Jump in and die and be the more alive."
*
1. Sikandar (Iskandar). Alexander the Great.

2. Khizar (Khizr). The prophet Khizr, who discovered and drank of the water of life, thereby becoming immortal. According to Oriental tradition, lie was a wazir of Iskandar. He is also regarded as the guardian spirit of the sea.
