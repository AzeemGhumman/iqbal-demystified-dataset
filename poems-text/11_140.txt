دلی برکف نہادم ، دلبری نیست
*
دلی برکف نہادم ، دلبری نیست
متاعی داشتم ، غارتگری نیست
درون سینۂ من منزلی گیر
مسلمانی ز من تنہا تری نیست
*
XI-LXII
*


*
I hold a heart in hand find not a beau,
A treasure I have but no robbers Lo;
I pray thee to make in my heart a place,
How much lonely I am there is no case. $1

*
1.         Lonely (adj. ) means alone, solitary, isolated, unhappy as being alone, longing for friends' company. Lonesome (adj. ) having or causing a lonely feeling, unfrequented, desolate n. (colloq.); self, as all by my lonesome. The readers may use lonesome or lonely as they like.
