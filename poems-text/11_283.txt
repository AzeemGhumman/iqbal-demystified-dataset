زمانہ فتنہ ہا  ورد و بگذشت
*
زمانہ فتنہ ہا  ورد و بگذشت
خسان را در بغل پرورد و بگذشت
دو صد بغداد را چنگیزی او
چو گور تیرہ بختان کرد و بگذشت
*
II-I
*


*
With times came unrest which passed so quick,
It reared great satans and passed so quick.
Many Baghdads were raped by tyrants greal,
It levelled their graves and passed like spate. $1

*
1          By Ghenhis (gez) Khan,the Mangol conquerer of land from Black Sea to Pacific (1162 to 1227), pronounced in Persian (Changaiz Khan).
