عرب را حق دلیل کاروان کرد
*
عرب را حق دلیل کاروان کرد
کہ او با فقر خود را امتحان کرد
اگر فقر تہی دستان غیور است
جہانے را تہ و بالا توان کرد
*
VIII-II
*


*
The Truth chose Arab for caravan’s lead,
On faqr since he tested his own self’s breed.
If the poor’s content with envy is green,
His growth can upset the whole world’s scene. $1

*
1.         Here green means flourishing, in 'figurative' sanse. It means full of vigour as we say (keep a man's memory green). He lives to a green old age, although looking green with envy also means pale or sickly looking.
