بگو از من بہ پرویزان این عصر
*
بگو از من بہ پرویزان این عصر
نہ فرہادم کہ گیرم تیشہ در دست
ز خاری کو خلد در سینۂ من
دل صد بیستون را میتوان خست
*
I-VII
*


*
To tyrants of this age I would thus say,
An axe I use not in the Farhad’s way.
From the prick of thorn I cherish in heart,
A mountain’s heart too could be torn apart. $1

*
1.         In text Iqbal says 'Pervez' of this age, a title of the old kings of Persia, tyrants of that age. In Persian legends Farhad was the lover of Shirin,  a princess of Persia, who was entrusted the task of making a canal from a mountain (called Baestoon).
