من اندر مشرق و مغرب غریبم
*
من اندر مشرق و مغرب غریبم
کہ از یاران محرم بی نصیبم
غم خود را بگویم با دل خویش
چہ معصومانہ غربت را فریبم
*
XI-XLVIX
*


*
So alien I am in West and East,
No confidants true I have the least.
To heart I’m telling my sorrowful mood,
To cheat my solitude, like child I brood. $1

*
1.         This is Iqbal's soliloquy to himself, when he is feeling himself alone without alter egos (friends) to have a tete-a-tete talk. Here alien means lonely. (Soliloquy; talk to one's self.)
