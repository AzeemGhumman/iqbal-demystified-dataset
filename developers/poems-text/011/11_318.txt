گہی جویندۂ حسن غریبے
*
گہی جویندۂ حسن غریبے 
خطیبی منبر او از صلیبی
گہی سلطان با خیل و سپاہی
ولی از دولت خود بی نصیبی
*
VIII-VIII
*


*
To that rare beauty my heart seeks again,
To speaker $1 whose pulpit was ‘cross’ and slain.
To the King $2 often with lancers and force,
To wealth who never touched at any course.



*
1.         Viz. Mansoor Hallaj (cotton carder), though Mansoor Hallaj was never a cotton carder by profession.

2.         Viz. Sultan Noor-ud-Din Zangi. In this quatrain Iqbal has used a most laconic language as he had explained tales of two great heroes.
