سحر با ناقہ گفتم نرم تر رو
*
سحر با ناقہ گفتم نرم تر رو
کہ راکب خستہ و بیمار و پیر است
قدم مستانہ زد چندان کہ گوئی
بپایش ریگ این صحرا حریر است
*
III-I
*


*
That dawn I asked naqah not to run quick,
Its rider is feeble, too old and sick.
In a dancing prance move but she runs more $1 ,
That sand to her feet is a silky floor. $2

*
1. (Literal) In a gaily way trot but she would say,

2. To her desert sand is a silky way. This is why she likes to run fast. That soil of Yasrib and Mecca is also dear to her!
