بیا ساقی نقاب از رخ برافکن
*
بیا ساقی نقاب از رخ برافکن
چکید از چشم من خون دل من
بہ آن لحنی کہ نہ شرقی نہ غربی است
نوائی از مقام ’’لاتخف‘‘ زن
*
I-IV
*


*
Come O’ bearer and raise the veils aside,
Cause my heart’s blood’ dripping from the eyes side.
From a tone which gives no East or. West trace,
Send a ‘no fear note’ from the ‘no fear place.’ $1

*
1.         Line 3: here East or West mean Eastern or Western (tune). Line 4, Send a 'La takhaf' note from its place, was the best literal translation (Latakhaf means fear not) as it was addressed to Moses by God.
