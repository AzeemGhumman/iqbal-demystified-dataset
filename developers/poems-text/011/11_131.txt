درین عالم بہشت خرمی ہست
*
درین عالم بہشت خرمی ہست
بشاخ او ز اشک من نمی ہست
نصیب او ہنوز آن ہای و ہو نیست
کہ او در انتظار آدمی ہست
*
XI-LIII
*


*
The world has charms like paradise true;
My tears give a moist to the shoots too.
She lacks to her part that cry and hue,
She’s looking for a man for guidance true. $1

*
1.         Basic thought: The nation is not in a position to cause a revolution in this world. She is too weak for any important activity (for a cataclysmic change).
