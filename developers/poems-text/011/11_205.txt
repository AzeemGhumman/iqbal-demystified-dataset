بہ روی عقل و دل بگشای ہر در
*
بہ روی عقل و دل بگشای ہر در
بگیر از پیر ہر میخانہ ساغر
’’دران کوش از نیاز سینہ پرور
کہ دامن پاک داری  ستین تر‘‘
*
VI-IV
*


*
For sense and heart’s sake leave each door ajar,
Take a cup thus from every sect’s bar.
Make all the efforts with love and heart pure,
To lead a chaste life with no greed and lure $1

*
1.         Line 3, has been taken from a verse of Amir Khisro (viz. Khawaja Abul-Hassan Amir Khisro) known as Nightingale of India (Tooti-e-Hind). He introduced a new branch of Urdu poetry.
